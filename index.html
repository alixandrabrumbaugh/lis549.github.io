Directions

From Newark Liberty International Airport

  • Get on I-78 Express E/Phillipsburg-Newark Expy from Hotel Rd.
  • Follow I-78 E to Varick St in Manhattan, New York. Take Exit 4 from Holland Tunnel.
  • Continue on Varick St. Take Worth St and E Broadway to Canal St.

From JFK International Airport

  • Get on I-678 N from 130th Pl.
  • Take Exit 2 to Van Wyck Expy. Take Exit 2 from I-678 N.
  • Get on I-678 N/Van Wyck Expy.
  • Continue on I-678 N/Van Wyck Expy. Take Grand Central Pkwy, I-495 W, Queens Midtown Tunnel and FDR Dr to South St in Manhattan. Take Exit 4 from FDR Dr.
  • Continue on South St. Take Pike St to Canal St.
