Cassandra Cain

Cassandra Cain first appeared in the main Batman series as part of the historic "No Man's Land" event, designed and led by cartoonist Jordan B. Gorfinkel. This event, in which the U.S. government quarantines and cuts all ties with Gotham City following a magnitude 7.6 earthquake, had effects that reverberated throughout the Batman universe. Of greatest relevance here is the introduction of Cass Cain, who is portrayed as a mute courier under the tutelage of Barbara Gordon (formerly Batgirl). As the NML storyline progressed, Cass was revealed to be the daughter of infamous assassin David Cain
